Rootbeer Simple Syrup

Enjoy a delicious Cold Brew Float! Our classic cold brew mixed with our brand new Rootbeer Syrup is topped with vanilla sweet cream cold foam to create a sweet treat for those warm summer days. The secret to the syrup? Molasses and Wintergreen extract create a flavor profile like that of a cold sasparilla.

Syrup Ingredients

  • 2.5 Cups Dark Brown Sugar
  • 1.5 Cups Hot Water
  • 2 Teaspoons Wintergreen Extract
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract

Directions

  1. Combine brown sugar and hot tower water in a cambro Whisk to combine.
  2. Stir in the wintergreen and vanilla extracts.
  3. Cool, bottle, and store in the frisge for up to one month.
