Apple Cider Syrup

A chai latte crafted with our housemade apple cider syrup and Masala Chai concentrate.

Our spiced Masala Chai latte is paired with our apple cider syrup to create an excellent fall beverage. We mix our Masala Chai concentrate with a milk of your choice, a few pumps of apple cider syrup, steam it latte style, then serve it with a sprinkle of cinnamon on top!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Apple Cider
  • 2 Cups packed Brown Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Diamond Kosher Salt
  • 2 Cinnamon Sticks

Preparation

  1. Combine apple cider and brown sugar in a pot on a hotplate.
  2. Bring Mixture to a boil.
  3. Add cinnamon sticks, then reduce to a high simmer.
  4. Simmer for 15 minutes, or until the mixture begins to boil over.
  5. Remove from heat, add your salt, and stir.
  6. Strain mixture into a cambro with a fine mesh strainer.
  7. Cool, bottle, and date syrup for up to 1 month.
