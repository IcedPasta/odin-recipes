Mango Bergamot Syrup

A refreshing iced latte made with a mango infused bergamot black tea syrup. The secret is to brew the tea in the simple syrup itself, so that all of the flavors meld into the final product.

Syrup Ingredients

  • 1 Bag Firepot Mango Bergamot Black Tea
  • 4 Cups Cane Sugar
  • 2 Cups Honey
  • 6 Cups Filtered Water
  • 1 Teaspoon Kosher Diamond Salt

Directions

  1. Combine water, sugar, and honey on a hot plate. Bring to a boil.
  2. Once boiling, reduce to a simmer and add your tea to the micture. Brew for 4 Minutes.
  3. Once your tea has brewed, strain the tea leaves out with a fine mesh strainer into a cambro.
  4. Add your salt, cool the mixture, then bottle it.
  5. Store for up to 1 month in the fridge.
